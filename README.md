## NAVIGATION NOTES FOR CHAMPLAIN CANAL CRUISERS

**Starting Point:** Whitehall Marina (Lock 12) is at Mile 0.0 - the official gateway from the Champlain Canal to Lake Champlain.

**Key Distance Milestones:**
- **Mile 0-35:** Southern Lake (narrow, scenic section past Crown Point)
- **Mile 35-65:** Mid-Lake widening (Charlotte to Burlington area - lake opens up)
- **Mile 65-85:** Broader Lake (Burlington/Colchester to Plattsburgh - widest sections)
- **Mile 85-100:** Islands Region (Grand Isle, North Hero, and approaches to Canada)

**Distance Calculation Method:** Distances shown are straight-line measurements (as the crow flies). Actual navigation distances will be 10-20% longer due to:
- Following navigation channels
- Natural curves of the lake
- Avoiding shallow areas
- Weather-related course adjustments

**Fuel Stops:** Fuel is available at 33 marinas (70% of total). Major fuel stop clusters at:
- Mile 33-34 (Port Henry area)
- Mile 64-69 (Burlington/Colchester - multiple options)
- Mile 87-88 (Islands region)
- Mile 99 (Rouses Point - last fuel before Canada)

**Overnight Planning:** With 100 miles from Whitehall to the Canadian border, most cruisers plan:
- **One overnight:** Stop around Mile 50-65 (Burlington area)
- **Two overnights:** Stop at Mile 33 (Port Henry) and Mile 70 (Colchester)
- **Three overnights:** Add stop at Mile 88 (Islands region)
