## Overview
This comprehensive directory includes **47 marinas** across Lake Champlain, covering both New York and Vermont shorelines from south to north. The directory includes full-service marinas, yacht clubs with marina services, state park marinas, and commercial operations.

## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **Pricing Currency:** All prices listed in USD
2. **Contact for Current Rates:** Most marinas recommend calling for current seasonal slip rates, winter storage rates, and service pricing as rates change annually
3. **Reservations:** Highly recommended for peak season (July-August) and holiday weekends. Burlington area and Islands area fill quickly.
4. **Pump-Out Services:** Vermont Clean Vessel Act provides free or low-cost pump-out at most Vermont marinas. Many marinas offer complimentary pump-out with fuel purchase.
5. **BoatUS Discounts:** Available at select marinas (typically 10% off transient rates and fuel discounts)
6. **Safe Harbor Network:** Three Safe Harbor marinas on lake offer membership benefits with access to entire Safe Harbor network nationwide
7. **Fuel Notes:** Non-ethanol gasoline (typically 91 octane) widely available. Diesel available at most full-service marinas. Fuel prices vary; seasonal slip holders often receive discounts.
8. **Winter Storage:** Most marinas offer both indoor and outdoor winter storage. Indoor heated storage limited and books early (Mooney Bay has largest capacity).
9. **VHF Monitoring:** Most marinas monitor VHF Channel 9 and/or 16 during operating season
10. **Accessibility:** Most modern facilities offer ADA-accessible restrooms, showers, and docks. Contact individual marinas for specific accessibility features.
11. **Pet Policies:** Most marinas are dog-friendly. Burton Island State Park restricts pets from swimming area and marina. Always check with individual marina.
12. **Payment Methods:** Most marinas accept major credit cards (VISA, MasterCard, Discover, AMEX) and cash. Some municipal marinas also accept debit cards.
13. **Cancellation Policies:** Typically 24-72 hours notice required for transient reservations. Seasonal slip deposits often non-refundable.
14. **Liveaboard:** Not all marinas permit liveaboard. Check with individual marina for policies (The Moorings Marina confirmed liveaboard allowed).

This comprehensive directory represents all identified full-service marinas, yacht clubs with marina services, state park marinas, and commercial marina operations on Lake Champlain as of November 2025. Simple boat launches without marina services have been excluded per request. Contact information, services, and pricing are based on publicly available information and official marina sources. Mariners should always call ahead to confirm current availability, rates, and services.
